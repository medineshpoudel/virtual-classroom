About

Mr. Sushil Shrestha is an Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science and Engineering. He is also a Lead Researcher of Digital Learning Research Lab. He has done several researches in different fields like: Human Computer Interaction, Data Mining, Learning Analytics, Online Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Knowledge Management and Cognitive Science. He has extensive experiences of presenting in several international platforms both inside and outside country. He is involved in several research projects.

Achievments

His major teaching courses are: Human Computer Interaction, Data Mining. Programming, Knowledge Management and Information System.

My Objective

To provide Knowledge to produce usable and safe systems, as well as functional systems and to produce computer systems with good usability, develop tools and techniques to enable building suitable systems.
